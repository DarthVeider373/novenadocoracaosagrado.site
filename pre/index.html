About Us

Salon-Quality Nail Care, Right Here in Barnet

Red Carpet Manicure UK Limited has been perfecting the art of beautiful nails since 2011. From our studio on Moon Lane in Barnet, our friendly, fully-trained technicians deliver treatments that feel as luxurious as they look.

We use only professional-grade products and rigorous hygiene standards, so you can relax knowing you're in safe, expert hands. Whether it's a quick tidy-up before work or a full pamper session, we'll help you leave feeling wonderful.
